About us

Service expert provides a platform that allows skilled and experienced professionals to connect with users looking for specific services. All the professionals, though experienced and skilled, undergo intensive training modules before being allowed to list their services on the platform. Once on the platform, our match-making algorithm identifies professionals who are closest to the users’ requirements and available at the requested time and date.
